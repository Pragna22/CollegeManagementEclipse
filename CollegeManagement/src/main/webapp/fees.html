FEES STRUCTURE

➢  The students admitted into this institute are selected through Joint Entrance Examination (Main) and a state level entrance test conducted by the West Bengal Joint Entrance Examination Board (WBJEE). Depending on the rank obtained at JEE (Main) / WBJEE and the preference of the candidates, the Central Selection Committee for Engineering & Technology allots the candidates for admission into this institute strictly according to merit. The students are also admitted directly in second year through JELET counselling in case of diploma level students.
